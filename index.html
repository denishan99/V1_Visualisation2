World Food Dynamics

Global Insights and Australia's Role

This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of global
food production trends , illustrating the dynamics and variations
across different countries. It emphasizes Australia’s vital role
in the agricultural sector, showcasing its contributions to
the global food supply and production. Users can explore
data across multiple years to understand trends and impacts.

Australia's Food Consumption



Insights into Australia’s food consumption trends.
It highlights key changes over recent years, including declines
in legumes, fruit, and vegetables. Despite these drops,
dairy substitutes and snack foods have shown growth. Users
can explore data from 2018-19 to 2022-23 to understand
how eating habits have evolved.





Australia's nutrient consumption data for the year 2022-2023.
This chart presents information on protein, carbohydrates,
total fat, and dietary fiber levels consumed.
Analyzing these nutrients helps understand dietary habits
and offers insights for improving public health.
